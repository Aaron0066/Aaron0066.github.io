Website Evaluations

Fly Hyer

Fly Hyer is a premium private jet booking service with a sleek and modern design.

  • Contrast: The site uses a dark theme with gold accents, making content visually striking.
  • Repetition: Fonts, buttons, and spacing are consistent throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Elements are well-aligned, giving it a professional and structured appearance.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together, improving readability and navigation.

Yale School of Art

The Yale School of Art website is chaotic and hard to navigate, lacking a clear design structure.

  • Contrast: Poor color choices and inconsistent text formatting make it difficult to read.
  • Repetition: No consistent styles for fonts, spacing, or navigation.
  • Alignment: Many elements appear randomly placed with no proper grid system.
  • Proximity: Sections of content are scattered, making it hard to follow the information flow.
